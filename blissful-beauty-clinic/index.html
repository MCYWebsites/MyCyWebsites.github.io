About the Clinic

A small Limassol clinic, built for regulars.

Blissful Beauty Clinic was built around one belief — that beauty care, done properly, is a kindness. To your skin, to your day, and to yourself.

We keep our rooms quiet, our diary generous, and our treatments matched carefully to the person in front of us. The result is a clinic that feels less like an appointment and more like an hour you give yourself.

Most of our work comes from people who've been before — and bring someone with them next time.
