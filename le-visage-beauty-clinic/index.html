Our Treatments

The art of looking after the face.

Treatments are matched to your skin and your day — never to a fixed menu. Every session begins with a quiet conversation.

i.

Signature Facials

Deep cleansing, hydration, and skin-renewal facials performed by hand — the kind that leave your skin visibly calmer for days afterwards.

ii.

Aesthetic Care

Subtle, considered aesthetic treatments — never overdone, always honest. We'll only suggest what truly suits you.

iii.

Body Rituals

Therapeutic body treatments and massage — for those moments when looking after the face means looking after everything else, too.

iv.

Skin Diagnostics

Skin analysis sessions designed to understand the cause of what you see — so the treatment plan addresses it, not just the surface.

Signature Rituals

Three rituals our regulars return for.

i

The Bright Hour

Our brightening facial — a one-hour ritual to revive a tired, dull complexion before an event, holiday, or simply for yourself.

ii

The Calm Hour

A quiet, restorative facial for sensitive or stressed skin — gentle hands, cool products, and an unhurried pace.

iii

The Renewal

An extended ritual combining face, neck and décolletage — designed for visible, lasting renewal.
